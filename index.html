About us

Once upon a time, there was a smoker so powerful that no man could contain it. Legend says that smoker could summon the BBQ gods and they would bless your meat with their smoke. That smoker was tamed by one man, Good-n-Smoked's head chef Frank Tagnus. Now Frank fulfills his destiny everyday of treating people to the best damn smoked BBQ in the world.
